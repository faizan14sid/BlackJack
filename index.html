Rules

Players are dealt with a starting hand of 2 cards with the hopes of getting the magical no. of 21, or to get as close to 21 as possible.If a player gets 21 on his initial hand,it is called "blackjack" The other ways to win are to hold a higher sum of cards at the end of the play than the other player.
